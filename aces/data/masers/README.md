# Maser catalogs

Maser catalogs bundled for crossmatching against ACES continuum sources
(see `aces/analysis/crossmatch_masers.py`, entry point `aces_crossmatch_masers`).

All files share a uniform schema and are (re)generated by
`aces/analysis/prepare_maser_catalogs.py`:

    ra_deg, dec_deg, vlsr [km/s], flux, flux_unit, name, species, catalog, reference

| File | Species | Rows | Coverage | Source |
|------|---------|------|----------|--------|
| `h2o_walsh2014.fits` | H2O | 2790 | all-plane | Walsh et al. 2014, MNRAS 442, 2240 (ATCA water masers) |
| `h2o_lu2019_cmz.fits` | H2O | 23 | **CMZ** | Lu et al. 2019, ApJS 244, 35 table4 (CMZ water masers) |
| `ch3oh_cotton2016_cmz.fits` | CH3OH | 2240 | **CMZ** | Cotton & Yusef-Zadeh 2016, ApJS 227, 10 (VLA 36 GHz class-I; VizieR `J/ApJS/227/10`) |
| `ch3oh_glostar.fits` | CH3OH | 2904 | all-plane | Nguyen et al. 2022, A&A 666, A59 (GLOSTAR 6.7 GHz class-II; VizieR `J/A+A/666/A59`) |

The **Cotton & Yusef-Zadeh (2016)** 36 GHz class-I catalog is the dense
CMZ-specific methanol maser survey (all 2240 spots lie in the ACES footprint);
the all-plane GLOSTAR/Walsh catalogs undersample the CMZ on their own.

In addition, the **SWAG 22 GHz water masers** (Ward, Ott & Meier, in prep.; the
dense CMZ H2O survey) are **not** bundled here — they are read from disk at
runtime (`crossmatch_masers._load_swag_masers`, `SWAG_DIR`) when the
machine-readable tables are present, and skipped otherwise.

These are published catalogs redistributed here for reproducibility; cite the
original references when using them.
